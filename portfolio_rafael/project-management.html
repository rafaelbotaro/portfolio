PROJECT MANAGEMENT

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

This project consisted in webdesign and wordpress development.

From 2015 to 2016 I worked as a marketing manager at Pacific Star Panamerica, a distributor of Beghelli's Led Bulbs in Guayaquil, Ecuador. I was responsible for creating the company's brand, website and e-mail marketing campaigns.

Dental Cord is a dentist office located in La Troncal, Ecuador. This project consists in the development of their website in Wordpress. Clients can make appointments online and the appointments can be managed through the website's back office.
